Above there are 3 examples of graphs outputted from the AI. From alpha testing, I noticed that with a larger time frame, the graphs look a lot more in depth. This could be because my AI is based on historical data and not live, hourly data. That's why I decided to have graphs from one year ago, two years ago and the whole time period ROSE has been around. Graph 1 has only 'accual' plotted because the AI predicts these results using the historical data prior to the time frame, therefor since it's from the beginning there is no historical data to make predictions from. Having a graphs with just the accual results on it can also make it clearer to view alongside the other graphs. On the X axis, there are the days and on the Y axis, there is the value of the ROSE.
