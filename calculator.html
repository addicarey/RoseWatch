More Information


This calculator is made using a simple JavaScript Function. It uses a CoinGecko API which fetches the latest ROSE prices. It then converts the amount specified to the ROSE price and outputs the result underneath. The conversion is slightly inaccurate due to some additional charges or transaction fees that are not reflected in the exchange rate, which only slightly changes the conversion. Otherwise, it is quite accurate. The ROSE price now averages at around €0.05. The price was at its highest back on January 15th 2022, where the price was €0.54. Its lowest was on November 26th 2020, where the price was €0.03. To learn more feel free to visit the about page. For further information feel free to check out CoinMarketCap or CoinGecko.
